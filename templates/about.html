About Pinless

Pinless is a privacy-focused frontend for Pinterest. It lets you browse public Pinterest content without connecting to Pinterest directly from your browser.

Frequently asked questions

I want to report abuse

Pinless does not host the underlying content. It only proxies content that is already public on Pinterest. If something needs to be removed, report it to Pinterest directly.

Why use cookies?

Pagination tokens in URLs can leak into browser history, referrers, and server logs. Session cookies are the more private option here.
